Built different: no server, no signup, no data collection

Every other ITR tool asks you to upload your Form 16, broker P&L, or 26AS to their servers — or log in to your broker account directly. ITRready doesn't. All parsing and tax computation happens locally in your browser using JavaScript; your data is saved only in your browser's local storage and is never transmitted anywhere. Close the tab, and nothing about your finances has touched the internet.
